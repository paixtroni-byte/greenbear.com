GreenBear is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental protection, ecological awareness, and the promotion of sustainable practices within society. Its mission is to inform and empower the public on crucial environmental issues such as climate change, wildlife conservation, reforestation, and the responsible management of natural resources.

With a focus on people, the environment, and animals, GreenBear designs and implements educational, social, and ecological initiatives, including awareness campaigns, experiential programs, volunteer activities, and collaborations with schools, communities, and other organizations. It also develops digital content and online initiatives aimed at strengthening environmental education and encouraging eco-friendly solutions in everyday life.

As a non-profit organization, GreenBear does not seek financial profit. It relies on citizen participation, social contribution, and financial support through donations.
